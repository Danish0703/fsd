Experiment-8: MongoDB Count, Limit, Sort, and Skip Operations

This experiment details how to use count, limit, sort, and skip operations in MongoDB.

mongodb_operations.txt:

use MYDB2
db.createCollection("employees")
db.employees.insertMany([{id:111, name:\'aaaa\', salary:10000},{id:222, name:\'bbbb\', salary:15000},{id:333, name:\'cccc\', salary:12000},{id:444, name:\'dddd\', salary:17000}])
db.employees.find().pretty()
db.employees.find().count()
db.employees.find({salary:10000}).count()
db.employees.find().pretty().limit(1)
db.employees.find().pretty().limit(2)
db.employees.find().pretty().skip(2)
db.employees.find().pretty().skip(2).limit(2)
db.employees.find().sort({id:1})
db.employees.find().sort({id:-1})
